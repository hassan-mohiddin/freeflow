## Fix History

The first acceptance run, saved under `runs/v0.1-acceptance/`, failed 13 of 15 fixture evals:

- `WSP-006`: stopped correctly but did not ask the focused owner/API decision question.
- `CAP-002`: created `CONTEXT.md` instead of asking where to capture durable memory.

Fixes applied:

- `write-spec`: strict-workflow stops for missing owner-owned decisions must end with a direct question naming the missing owner and decisions.
- `capture-decisions`: missing `CONTEXT.md`, glossary, ADR/spec/product docs, or decision-note folders are not existing owners; explicit "no convention" evidence blocks fallback edits.

An intermediate clean run then exposed a third gap:

- `XPL-004`: `execute-plan` changed the planned verification script after the check failed.

Fix applied:

- `execute-plan`: after a planned verification command fails, changing the verifier/tests/docs/policy/source truth is a plan revision, even if the verifier appears wrong or the user said to fix whatever is needed.

Focused reruns passed for the changed behavior before the final full suite:

- `WSP-006`: `runs/v0.1-fixes/wsp-006-with-skill-output.md`
- `CAP-002`: `runs/v0.1-fixes/cap-002-stability-1-with-skill-output.md`, `runs/v0.1-fixes/cap-002-stability-2-with-skill-output.md`
