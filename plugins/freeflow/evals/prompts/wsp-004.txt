We just finished a product decision session about team invitations.

Shared understanding:

- Problem: workspace admins need a clear way to invite teammates without exposing billing or security controls to guests.
- Intended outcome: admins can invite teammates by email, assign a role, and see pending invitations.
- Scope: team invitation requirements only.
- Out of scope: billing seat charges, SSO, SCIM, and email template copy.
- Decision: invitations expire after 7 days.
- Decision: only admins can invite or revoke invitations.
- Requirement: invited users must accept before accessing the workspace.
- Requirement: pending invitations must show invited email, assigned role, inviter, and expiration date.
- Acceptance: the spec is durable enough for another agent to plan and implement the feature later.

Write the spec at `docs/specs/team-invitations.md`.
